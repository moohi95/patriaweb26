Why Type IV?

What is a Type IV Pressure Vessel?

Type IV pressure vessels use a full-composite construction with a polymer liner — offering the best combination of safety, weight, and performance.

Lightweight Construction

Carbon fibre composite wrapping delivers a vessel that is 60% lighter than steel or aluminium equivalents — reducing vehicle weight and improving fuel efficiency.

Enhanced Safety

The polymer liner combined with multi-directional carbon fibre reinforcement ensures structural integrity and safety under extreme pressure conditions.

Corrosion Resistance

Unlike metal cylinders, composite vessels do not corrode, ensuring a longer lifespan and lower total cost of ownership for operators.

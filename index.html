How we do it

Precision Technology

We use filament winding — wrapping carbon fiber strands around a mandrel with precision — to produce lightweight, high-strength Type IV vessels. Combined with the composite curing oven and in-house burst testing — our production meets the highest industry standards.

  • Filament Winding
  • Carbon Fiber & Epoxy Resin
  • Composite Curing Oven
  • Burst Test Facility
